# Product Specification

## Vision

Sovereign Publishing Platform is an open-core, composable, cloud-agnostic, multi-device publishing platform for every schema.org CreativeWork type.

It supports independent authoritative opinions, open journals, research preprints, commercial knowledge products, community publishing, and enterprise-grade governance.

## Core personas

- Author
- Researcher
- Reviewer
- Editor
- Publisher
- Community moderator
- Subscriber
- Institution admin
- Tenant admin
- Registry publisher
- Commercial seller
- Developer

## Core jobs

- Create trustworthy CreativeWorks.
- Collaborate with reviewers and editors.
- Publish across devices and channels.
- Preserve provenance and version history.
- Discover, cite, syndicate, and monetize work.
- Run a publication or journal with governance.
- Install composable modules from a registry.
- Operate on a single-node Kubernetes cluster.

## Editions

### Community edition

- Authoring
- Publishing
- Schema.org JSON-LD
- Basic workflows
- Tenant isolation
- Registry client
- GitOps deployment
- Single-node operation

### Commercial edition

- Advanced governance
- Enterprise SSO and SCIM
- Advanced analytics
- Legal hold and retention
- Enterprise moderation
- Premium AI workflows
- Paid distribution automation
- Managed support

## Differentiators

- Schema.org-native content graph.
- Database-native workflow and audit model.
- GitOps-native operations.
- Open journal and preprint support.
- Registry-first composability.
- UCP-aligned commerce model.
- Web3-aligned provenance without forced speculation.
- Multi-tenant privacy and governance from day one.
